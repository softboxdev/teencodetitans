Welcome to TeenCode Titans! Our Olympiad Programming Courses for children aged 12-18 are designed to provide the best environment for young minds to develop their potential and prepare for a successful future. At TeenCode Titans, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that focuses on building strong foundations in algorithmic thinking, problem-solving skills, and advanced programming techniques.

  • Group 1 (Ages 12-14)
  • Group 2 (Ages 15-18)

Schedule: Weekdays: Tuesday and Thursday, 16:00 - 17:30, 17:30 - 19:00

Preparation

In this group, we focus on building foundational programming skills. Students will learn basic algorithms, data structures, and coding best practices through hands-on projects and interactive lessons.

Topics

Examples of topics include Introduction to Python, Basic Algorithms, Data Structures, and Problem Solving Techniques.

Outcomes

By the end of the course, students will be able to develop simple applications, solve basic algorithmic problems, and understand fundamental programming concepts. They will also be prepared to tackle more advanced topics in the future.

Schedule: Weekdays: Tuesday and Thursday, 19:00 - 20:30

Preparation

This group is designed to deepen students' knowledge in programming and algorithmic thinking. We cover more advanced topics and provide a robust preparation for programming competitions and further studies in computer science.

Topics

Examples of topics include Advanced Algorithms, Competitive Programming, Object-Oriented Programming, and Data Structures.

Outcomes

Students will be able to solve complex algorithmic problems, participate in programming competitions, and develop advanced applications. They will be well-prepared for university-level computer science courses and careers in technology.
